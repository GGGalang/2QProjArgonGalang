ABOUT US

The PASAMA Project is quite literally a project, designed by a 14-year-old for his Computer Science class. This was a vision he had, one that he quite well knew that would serve some practical use if actually driven to completion, as it would serve as a good platform for aggregating news sources for viewers and users alike to get summaries of news quickly and unbiased, from different sources and thus different views.
